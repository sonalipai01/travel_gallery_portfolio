Italy (Rome, Amalfi Coast, Capri)

These are a few photos from my family trip to Italy in 2019. We first flew into Rome, where we toured the Colloseum and other ancient buildings. Then, we took a 3-hour drive down the coast of Italy, and finally, arrived in the Amalfi Coast. This coast is famous for its breathtaking views, cerulean waters, and cute little towns, like Positano.

Cancun, Mexico

In 2015, I traveled to Cancun, Mexico with my one of my best friends and her family. We stayed at an all-inclusive resort called Omni Cancun Hotel & Villas, which had several pools, volleyball courts, and lots of food. The resort was located right on the beach, so we had great views all the time. We also visited Xcaret, which was an adventure park about an hour away from the resort. The park had ziplining, swimming, tubing, shows, and more.
