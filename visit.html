Maldives

One place I've wanted to go to since I was young is the Maldives. Located in the north-central Indian Ocean, it consists of over 1,000 small islands, and is a very popular tourist destination. The resorts on the islands are famous for their luxury, with many of the hotels housing tourists in overwater villas -- some of which include water slides, pools, and direct access to the ocean.

Switzerland

There is no "perfect time" to visit Switzerland -- it is perfect year-round. The country looks like Winter Wonderland during the snowy months, and the Swiss Alps look like a green paradise during the warmer months. I've always dreamed of skydiving or paragliding in Interlaken, a mountainous resort town popular among many tourists.

Australia

Australia is home to an array of wildlife, adventures, and paradise. One of the most popular destinations in Australia is the Syndey Opera House, a performing arts center that hosts over 40 shows a week. You can also find many white-sand beaches, such as Bondi Beach, which draw surfers, swimmers, and sunbathers. You can't miss the exotic wildlife in Australia either, such as kangaroos, quokkas, koalas, and more.
